# autoexports.live Brand Identity & Strategy

## 1. Brand Essence

**autoexports.live** is a global vehicle procurement and export logistics platform. It is designed to feel like a high-authority "Trading Floor"â€”a place where high-value transactions happen with absolute transparency, speed, and security.

### Strategy: "Industrial Chic"

Unlike generic consumer marketplaces that use soft gradients and friendly blues, **autoexports.live** utilizes a "Grounded" design language.

- **Solid Blocks:** Favoring solid colors and sharp borders over blurred shadows.
- **Depth:** Using layered UI elements and high contrast to represent the multi-stage logistics journey.
- **Authority:** Professional typography and a palette that suggests security and "armor-plated" protection.

## 3. Topography & Typography

- **Headline Font:** Sora or Outfit (Geometric Sans-serif with a technical edge)
- **Body Font:** Inter (High legibility for data-heavy logistics tables)
- **Mono Font:** JetBrains Mono (Used for Lot Numbers, VINs, and Tracking IDs to emphasize precision)

---

## 4. Logo Direction

The logo should embody **Global Connectivity** and **Mechanical Speed**.

### Concepts:

1.  **The Globe + Bolt:** Combining a stylized Earth (global reach) with a lightning bolt (speed/EV focus).
2.  **The "Live" Node:** A pulse or live indicator integrated into the `.live` extension.
3.  **Monogram:** A robust "AE" block that looks like a shipping container or a vehicle chassis.

### Suggested Execution:

- **Wordmark:** `autoexports` in lowercase, bold, sans-serif. Use Deep Slate for `autoexports` and Amber Gold for `.live`.
- **Icon:** A geometric Globe icon with an internal arrow or "pulse" line.

---

## 5. UI Elements

- **Borders:** Use `2px` or `4px` solid borders for card components.
- **Shadows:** Use "Hard" shadows (e.g., `shadow-[8px_8px_0px_0px_rgba(15,23,42,0.1)]`) to create a physical, grounded feel.
- **Badges:** Rectangular, sharp corners, all-caps, extra letter spacing (e.g., `tracking-[0.2em]`).
