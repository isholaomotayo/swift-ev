# autoexports.live ‚Äî Brand, Copy & Design Specification

## Executive Summary

autoexports.live is a pioneering electric vehicle auction and import platform designed to connect African buyers (with initial focus on Nigeria) with Chinese EV manufacturers and dealers. The platform combines the proven auction mechanics of Copart with specialized services for EV imports including customs clearance, shipping logistics, and compliance documentation support.

## 1. Brand Identity

### 1.1 Brand Name
**autoexports.live**

The name combines "Volt" (representing electric power and energy) with "Bid" (the auction mechanism), clearly communicating the platform's dual value proposition: electric vehicles acquired through competitive bidding.

### 1.2 Tagline Options
- **Primary:** "Power Your Future. Bid Smart."
- **Secondary:** "Africa's Gateway to Electric Mobility"
- **Tertiary:** "From China to Your Driveway ‚Äî Electrified"

### 1.3 Brand Voice & Tone
The AutoExports brand speaks with authority on EV technology while remaining approachable and accessible to first-time importers. The voice balances professionalism with the excitement of auction participation.

**Voice Characteristics:**
- **Trustworthy:** We handle complex import processes so buyers don't have to
- **Innovative:** We're pioneering the African EV import market
- **Empowering:** We give buyers access to vehicles previously out of reach
- **Transparent:** No hidden fees, clear processes, honest vehicle assessments

### 1.5 Typography

**Primary Font Family:** Inter
- Headlines: Inter Bold (700), 32-48px
- Subheadings: Inter SemiBold (600), 20-24px
- Body: Inter Regular (400), 16px
- Captions: Inter Regular (400), 14px

**Secondary Font:** JetBrains Mono
- Used for: VIN numbers, lot numbers, bid amounts, technical specifications

### 1.6 Logo Specification

The AutoExports logo combines a stylized lightning bolt integrated into the letter "V" with clean, modern typography. The logo should work in the following variants:

- **Full Logo:** Icon + "autoexports.live" wordmark
- **Compact Logo:** Icon + "AutoExports" wordmark
- **Icon Only:** Lightning bolt V mark (for favicon, app icon)
- **Monochrome:** Single color version for limited print applications

**Logo Clearance:** Minimum spacing equal to the height of the "V" icon on all sides

## 9. Appendix: Nigerian EV Regulations Summary

### Current Incentives (as of 2025)
- EVs exempt from VAT (7.5%)
- EVs exempt from Import Adjustment Tax (IAT)
- Import duties: 10-20% (vs. 35% for ICE vehicles)
- 15% National Automotive Council (NAC) levy applies

### Government Targets
- 2033: 30% of vehicles sold to be locally manufactured EVs
- 2040: 100% zero-emission new car/van sales (ZEV Declaration)
- 2060: Net-zero emissions target

### Import Requirements
- Vehicles must be 2015 or newer (10-year age limit)
- SONCAP certification mandatory
- Form M through authorized Nigerian bank
- Certificate of Origin from exporting country

### Pending Legislation
- Electric Vehicle Transition and Green Mobility Bill (Senate review)
- Mandatory EV charging stations at fuel stations
- Additional incentives for local assembly partnerships
